# SQL Retail Sales Analysis

## Project Overview
This project performs end-to-end analysis on a retail sales database using SQL.  
It focuses on understanding business performance, customer behavior, and product performance using real-world reporting metrics.

The project simulates real business reporting scenarios used by Data Analysts.

---

## Database Tables
- Customers
- Orders
- Order_Items
- Products

---

## Business Analysis Performed

### Overall Business Metrics
- Total Customers
- Total Orders
- Total Revenue
- Monthly Revenue

---

### Customer Analysis
- Top 5 Customers by Revenue
- Top 5 Customers by Orders & Revenue
- Customers Who Never Ordered
- Customers Who Ordered Only Once
- Repeating Customers
- Total Amount Spent per Customer
- Average Order Value (AOV)

---

### Product Analysis
- Total Revenue per Product
- Total Quantity Sold per Product
- Products Never Sold
- Top 5 Products by Revenue
- Top 5 Products by Quantity Sold

---

### Time-Based Analysis
- Monthly Revenue
- Peak Sales Month
- Customer Purchase Behavior Over Time

---

## SQL Concepts Used
- Multi Table Joins
- Aggregations & Grouping
- Subqueries
- Conditional Filtering
- Sorting & Ranking
- Business KPI Calculations
